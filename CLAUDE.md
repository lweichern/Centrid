# Centrid Hub — Company Website

## Project Overview

Build a clean, professional 4-page company website for **Centrid Hub** — an interior design & build studio based in Selangor, Malaysia. They are an authorized **Signature Kitchen** partner (Malaysia's largest kitchen manufacturer since 1994).

## Tech Stack

- Next.js 14+ with App Router
- Tailwind CSS
- CSS transitions only (no Framer Motion)
- Deploy-ready on Vercel

## Brand

- **Company name:** Centrid Hub
- **Logo:** Located at `./public/logo.png` — blue icon with "CENTRID HUB" text
- **Colors:** Blue and white theme. Primary blue `#2563EB`, dark navy `#0F172A`, white backgrounds with soft blue tints
- **Tone:** Professional but creative — this is a design studio, the website itself should feel designed

## Pages (4 pages)

### 1. Homepage (`/`)
Hero section with a strong headline, company intro, featured projects showcase (use placeholder interior design images from Unsplash), services overview, stats section, and a CTA to contact.

**Services to display:**
- Interior Design
- Kitchen Design (Signature Kitchen partner)
- Renovation & Remodelling
- Space Planning & Consultation

**Stats section:**
- 150+ Projects Completed
- 6+ Years Experience
- 50+ Happy Clients
- 20+ Awards & Features

### 2. About Us (`/about`)
Company story (founded 2019, Selangor-based, 150+ projects), team/values section, what makes them different. Include a short section about the Signature Kitchen partnership — what it is and why it matters. Two-column layouts mixing text with images.

### 3. Portfolio (`/portfolio`)
Static project grid — no filtering, no category tabs, no detail modals. Each project card shows image, title, category, location, and year. Cards are display-only (no click interaction).

**Placeholder projects to use:**
- Setia Alam Bungalow — Residential, 3,200 sqft, Shah Alam, 2024
- Eco Sanctuary Semi-D — Residential, 2,800 sqft, Kota Kemuning, 2024
- Pinnacle Corporate HQ — Commercial, 6,500 sqft, Petaling Jaya, 2025
- Ara Greens Condo — Residential, 1,100 sqft, Ara Damansara, 2025
- The Artisan Cafe — Commercial, 1,800 sqft, SS2 PJ, 2025
- Signature Showroom — Kitchen, 3,600 sqft, Kota Kemuning, 2026

### 4. Contact Us (`/contact`)
No contact form. Display company contact info and a prominent WhatsApp CTA button. No Google Maps embed — just the address text.

**Contact details to display:**

- Email: info@centridhub.com
- Address: Selangor, Malaysia

## Navigation & Footer

**Navbar:** Logo on the left, nav links on the right — Home, About, Portfolio, Contact. Sticky on scroll. Hamburger menu on mobile.

**Footer:** Logo, quick links (Home, About, Portfolio, Contact), contact info (phone, email, address), copyright line ("© 2026 Centrid Hub. All rights reserved."), and a WhatsApp link.

## Features

- Floating WhatsApp button linking to `wa.me/60XXXXXXXXX`
- Simple CSS hover/fade transitions (no scroll-triggered animations)
- Fully mobile responsive with hamburger nav
- Basic meta titles per page (no per-page OG images)

## Creative Direction

Full creative freedom on layout and design details. Make it feel like a professional interior design studio website. Keep it blue/white themed and clean.
